## Overview
Interstellar explores profound themes around time, love, sacrifice, and humanity's relationship with the universe. Through Cooper's journey across space and time, the film reveals how our experience of time shapes who we become and why the journey through time matters as much as the destination.

## Core Concepts
- [[notes/The Paradox of Skipping Forward in Time|The Paradox of Skipping Forward in Time]] - Why wanting to time travel forward defeats its own purpose
- [[notes/The Bootstrap Paradox - Determinism and Free Will in Time Loops|The Bootstrap Paradox - Determinism and Free Will in Time Loops]] - How Cooper's future self creates the conditions for his present self, and what this means for free will
