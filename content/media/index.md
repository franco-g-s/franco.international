Notes and reviews of [[media/movies/Movies|movies]], [[media/youtube/YouTube|youtube videos]], [[media/books/Books|books]], and other media I've consumed.

## Rating System
Media is rated on a 7-point scale:
- **7** — Perfect (life-changing, must watch/read)  
- **6** — Excellent (highly recommend)
- **5** — Good (worth watching/reading)
- **4** — Passable (has merit)
- **3** — Bad (skip it)
- **2** — Atrocious (actively avoid)
- **1** — Evil (harmful)
