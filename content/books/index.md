# Books

My reading notes, reviews, and reflections on books I've read.

## Rating System

Books are rated on a 7-point scale:
- **7** — Perfect (life-changing, must read)
- **6** — Excellent (highly recommend)
- **5** — Good (worth reading)
- **4** — Passable (has merit)
- **3** — Bad (skip it)
- **2** — Atrocious (actively avoid)
- **1** — Evil (harmful)
