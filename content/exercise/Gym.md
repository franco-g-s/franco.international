### [[notes/Personal Gym Plan 2026|Gym Plan]]
My gym split is pretty much as basic as it gets and it isn't strict either. It has more or less developed organically based on what has worked and what has kept me going to the gym. Most of the ideas, strategies and methodologies come from [Jeff Nippard](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC68TLK0mAEzUyHx5x5k-S1Q), who I see as a gold standard for natural body building and exercise science.
